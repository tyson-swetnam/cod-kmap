Description:
Abstract:
This layer represents regions of the National Park Service (NPS). NPS is a bureau within the United States Department of the Interior. The organization consists of a headquarters office, seven regional offices and multiple park and support units.
Purpose:
To support coastal and ocean planning and other activities pursuant to the Energy Policy Act, Coastal Zone Management Act, Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act, National Environmental Policy Act, Rivers and Harbors Act and the Submerged Lands Act.

Process_Step:
Process_Description:
1. Download NPS Regions dataset from https://irma.nps.gov/App/Reference/Profile/1035889 2. Dissolve polygons based on NPS_Region field 3. Edit the 'NPS_Region' field name so it matches the data dictionary 4. Create 'mailingAdress' and 'telephoneNumber' fields and populate with information from http://www.nps.gov/aboutus/contactinformation.htm 5. Define all field aliases 6. create/update metadata 7. Determine symbology for layer file. Create a layer file containing symbology from each feature class. Include Layer Name, Description, Credits, and Scale Ranges (if applicable) within the layer file and its source. Save in same location data lives in.
